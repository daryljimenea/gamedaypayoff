Data Integrity from Major Upgrade

Who Does This Affect?  This will affect anyone that is currently using GameDay Payoff (Book and/or Player).

What Will Happen?  When you receive this update, you will be starting with a fresh database. That means that anything that you previously had will no longer be available to you.

Why Did This Happen?  If you have been using GameDay Payoff through the workaround provided, you probably know that we've been down a while upgrading our product to become compatible with Microsoft deployments. As part of this major upgrade to improve the performance of application, we needed to restructure the database. This restructuring of the way the database is written to and read from rendered the old one unusable.

What Can I Do About It?  When initially prompted for an update, HIT CANCEL. Initially, do not take this update. Instead, open up your application or continue as usual and retrieve all information that you need. Then only after you are finished, accept the update once you are prompted. To be prompted for an update again, you just restart the application. Once you accept, you will then be upgraded to the new Gameday Payoff application and begin using a new fresh database. You can then manually re-enter the information that you obtained prior to the upgrade. We sincerely apologize for you having to do this.

Do I Have to Worry About This Again?  Fortunately, you will never have to deal with this again. We were down as long as we were to make sure that this would never happen again. As far as subsequent upgrades go, your database created from this upgrade on will retain its integrity. This is a one shot deal. Once you do this manual re-entry into the upgraded application, you will not have to do it again on any subsequent upgrades.

What If I'm Not So Sure About How to Do This?  Please have no worries. Just drop us a line at gamedaypayoff@gmail.com and put "Data Integration Issue" in the subject line. We will get back with you as soon as possible. If you wish to be called, please put your number in the body of the email an indicate windows of time that would be best to reach you.

What If I Make a Mistake and Upgrade Before I Get The Data I Need?  It will be ok. Your data can absolutely still be retrieved, but it will take some doing. If you find yourself in this situation, please do not hesitate to reach us at gamedaypayoff@gmail.com and put "Data Integration Issue" in the subject line. We will get back with you as soon as possible. If you wish to be called, please put your number in the body of the email an indicate windows of time that would be best to reach you.
