fSports Wagering Terms

The difference between Place Wager and Record Wager is that with Place Wager, you are currently connected to a Book and you are directly placing that wager with your Book and it is also being recorded on your computer.  With Record Wager, you are not placing a wager directly with the Book, but are only recording it on your computer.  To accommodate, lines are editable from the Record Wager's screen, but not from the Place Wager screen.  The lines in the Place Wager screen are coming directly from the Book and are, therefore, not editable.

Also, a wager made through Place Wager that is placed with the Book and accepted cannot be edited or deleted.  A wager that is made through Record Wager can be edited or deleted. 

Use Place Wager screen for the following (Book must have a green ball associated with it):
  • Using a Book that is currently connected and the game you are wagering has not begun yet

    Use Record Wager screen for the following:
  • Using a Book for which you have never connected with
  • Using a Book for which you typically are connected with and use the Place Wagers screen, but communicated a wager to in a means other than using GameDay Payoff.  This applies to wagers for games that have not began yet or have expired (game has began or has ended)
  • Using a Book that is non-GameDay Payoff related.  If your Book is Las Vegas, the internet, or some other, GameDay Payoff can still be used for bookkeeping and real-time wager status.
