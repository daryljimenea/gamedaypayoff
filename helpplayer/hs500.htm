xAdd Book (bar book)

Use Add Book to enter any new Books that you want to manage through GameDay Payoff.  Upon hitting Add Book, you will be presented with a choice to add a Bar Book or a Personal Book.  The choice you make will take you down one of two paths that follows.

New Bar Book:  
Enter the name that you would like associated with that Book.  The name chosen can always be changed later through the Book Maintenance screen.  The balance is naturally set at $0.00 and cannot be changed.  When finished, hit Next.

You will then be required to Register with the Book for a Direct Connection.  In order for you to use a Bar Book, they must have sent you an invitation file and password to the email address that you provided to them when you signed up.  Assuming you have an invitation file and password from your Bar Book, hit the "Browse for invitation" button and find your ".gdp" file that the Book sent to you.  Then enter the password that the Bar Book provided to you and hit the "Next" button.  The password is case sensitive so make sure you enter it correctly.  If all went well, you will have finished the invite process and you now have a Direct Connection established with this Bar Book.  A congratulations will show and a "Notification" will pop up in the lower right of the screen indicating that you have received their Policy.  If the process fails, which is indicated by a "Connection Timeout", it means that your Bar Book is currently not available.  The Bar Book could be offline for some reason.  You should try again soon by repeating the process.  If you get an "Invalid password provided" error, re-enter your password (case sensitive) and try again.  If it still does not work, have your Book Retract that invitation and issue you another one.

New Personal Book:
There are two types of Personal Book's that GameDay Payoff supports.  One is a manual Book.  A manual Book is a Book for which you are not literally placing your wagers directly from your GameDay Payoff application to the Book's GameDayPayoff application.  Most likely, you communicate with this Book via the phone, email, or maybe a web browser.  They are most likely a local book, casino, or an internet based sportsbook.  Manually entering the wagers into GameDay Payoff when using these types of Book's allows you to track all wagers in real-time, evaluate positions, keep your accounting in line, and operate more efficiently.

Your other choice as to the type of Book to setup is that of a Direct Connection.  It is STRONGLY RECOMMENDED that you take advantage of this option.  You will realize all the benefits of a manual Book, but with 24/7 wagering, advanced wagering that only internet sportsbook's or Las Vegas offers, real-time wager status, controlled line movement, exposure management, persistent balance checking, and more.  Your Book must have GameDay Payoff to take advantage of the many Direct Connection features.  For more about Direct Connection, click on the "Why Use a Direct Connection?" link.

If you choose the Manual entry Book type,  you will be taken through a series of Policy screens.  These policy screens cover the limits, juice, offerings, rules, and odds for the wagers offered by your Book.  Pay careful attention to values that are pre-populated within the Policy screens.  The more common offerings were placed there for convenience reasons.  They may not reflect what your Book is offering and must be adjusted accordingly.  When Policy is complete, hit Finish and your Book will be created and ready for immediate use.

If you choose the Direct Connection Book type, you will be taken to a registry screen.  In order for you to use a Direct Connection Book, they must have GameDay Payoff and had sent you an invitation file and password.  Assuming you have an invitation file and password from your Book, hit the "Browse for invitation" button and find your ".gdp" file that the Book sent to you.  Then enter the password that he provided to you and hit the "Next" button.  The password is case sensitive so make sure you enter it correctly.  If all went well, you will have finished the invite process and you now have a Direct Connection with this Book.  A congratulations will show and a "Notification" will pop up in the lower right of the screen.  The notification lets you know whenever the Book changes their Policy on you.  Because that is such an important piece of information, you are informed of changes immediately.  When you establish your initial Direct Connection with the Book, this notification showed up because you received your initial Policy from them and you can go to Policy to view it.  If the process fails, which is indicated by a "Connection Timeout", it means that your Book is currently not available.  The Book could be offline or behind a firewall and unable to broadcast.  You should give the Book a call and resolve it.  If you get an "Invalid password provided" error, re-enter your password (case sensitive) and try again.  If it still does not work, have your Book Retract that invitation and issue you another one.

While changing the kind of book from Bar Book to Personal Book (or vise versa) is not possible, you can change a Personal Book from a Manual Book to a Direct Connection Book through the Book Maintenance screen.  However, once your Book is a Direct Connection Book, you cannot change them to a Manual Book.  You can still do everything with that Book accept change Policy (because that comes directly from the Book).  If you need your Direct Connection Book to be a Manual Book, you must delete that Personal Book and start over.  It is highly recommended that you write down the balance you have and any rules/odds settings before deleting.
