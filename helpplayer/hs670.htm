Games In Progress

This feature is only available in the Player version of GameDay Payoff.  At any time, you can come to this screen for all games that are currently being played.  The information on this screen is updated every 20 seconds.  In most cases, the lag time between actual events and our data being received by you is between 30 seconds and 5 minutes.  You can sort by league and choose to display one or more leagues at a time.

Sizing the screen (the application) will have a profound affect on how the games are arranged.  However, you cannot change the order in which the games are viewed.  The games appear on this screen with the Oldest games at the top and the newest games at the bottom.  When there is more than one column, they are arranged going across and down.

Also, it does not matter if you have a wager or not on the game.  It will be displayed here if it is currently being played.  Once a game becomes final, it will move from this screen to the box scores screen.

For college football and college basketball, you may see a "Non-Division I" for a team.  The reason being is that at this time, we are only supporting Division I teams.  Any team that is not a Division I team will not be designated by its name, but instead, they will all be designated by "Non-Division I".
