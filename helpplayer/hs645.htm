xVenue

The Venue section provides you with a line up of everything going on at the Bar as it relates to events and specials.  The categories available are as follows:  Daily Specials, Happy Hour, Bands/Music, General, Game Specials, and Televised Games.  If it's happening at the Bar, you'll see it here so it's a great source for you if you plan on going out.

The venue for a Bar is automatically brought up once for each time you choose a previously unchosen Bar within a session.  A session is each time you open the application.  That means that within a session, if you go back and forth between Bar Books, it will not keep appearing.  However, it will always appear the first time you bring a Bar Book up.

To make things a bit easier on you, the correct tab will automatically be chosen as associated with what day it is.  If it's Wednesday, the Wednesday tab will be chosen.  However, if you're looking ahead, feel free to choose another day to look at.  Every day of the week is available for your viewing.
