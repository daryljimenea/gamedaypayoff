xGeneral

This section is only active if you have chosen a Bar Book from the Book dropdown.  Once you choose a Bar Book and come to this section, all features within this section will pertain to that specific Bar and the offerings of that Bar.

The Bar specific features are as follows:
1)  Contests:  These are all the active contests currently going on with the specific Bar.
2)  Results:  This is the history of all the closed contests for a particular Bar.
3)  Venue:  This is a list of everything going on at the Bar related to specials and events. 
4)  Rules:  These are the rules of the contests for a particular Bar.
5)  Account Info:  This is your personal information that you have on record with the Bar. 
6)  Contact Us:  This is the contact information you will need if you have contest questions or technical questions.
7)  Sign Up a Bar:  Sign up a new Bar to GameDay Payoff - Bar and you will receive a years worth of free access to your Personal Books.  Bar Books are always free.
