Pay Apply

IMPORTANT:  To use GameDay Payoff - Player, it is required that you pay Sports Vice Solutions to gain access to your Books.  After your 10 day trial period, you will lose access to your Books.  Please use this screen to purchase access to your Books. Upon hitting the Buy Now button below, you will be immediately directed to PayPal's secure credit card processing website in order to complete the transaction. Through PayPal's secure credit card processing service, we accept ALL MAJOR CREDIT CARDS, DEBIT CARDS, AND PAYPAL PAYMENTS. Upon your payment being successfully processed, you will be notified as such through the email address that you are required to provide to PayPal. If you are running the Book application and the Player application from the same computer, you will only be required to make one purchase and it will be applied to both applications.

After you make a successful payment it is REQUIRED that you go to this screen within the application. If you have both applications on the same computer, you only need to do this once and it can be from either application. If you have both applications, but on different computers, you will need to pay twice and go through the "Pay Apply" screens on each application. After you submit the email address associated with your PayPal credit card processing payment, your expiration date for the Book lockout will be moved back accordingly and you will notice the change the NEXT TIME YOU OPEN THE APPLICATION. You MUST go through this process each and every time you make a payment to us.
