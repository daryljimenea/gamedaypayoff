Why Use a Direct Connection?

Quite simply, a Direct Connection is the optimal way to run your Book or be a Player.  Direct Connection is a communication tool that allows Books and Players to operate directly through the GameDay Payoff software application.  Direct Connection transmits and receives from one application to another directly through the internet without that transmission passing through our servers at any time.  By using Direct Connection, you and your Book/Player open your world to 24/7 wagering, advanced wagering that only internet sportsbook's or Las Vegas offers, controlled line movement, exposure management, and persistent balance checking.  What you eliminate by using a Direct Connection between you and your Book/Player is the hassle of phone communication, mistakes, minimal wager offerings, missed calls, blind-sided line changes, limited time slots to place wagers, and out of balance results.

You don't even have a reason to take off for baseball anymore.  Who cares that there's 20 games a day with complicated wager resolving?  The application will do it all for you.  Imagine holding shop 365 days a year and never picking up the phone or touching a calculator.

To experience this benefit, have your Book send you an Invite and then go to Add Book and select Direct Connection after assigning a name and opening balance.  Import the invitation file that your Book sent you, enter the password he provided to you, and then you are all set up and ready to go.  It's really that Easy!
