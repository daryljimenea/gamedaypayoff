Where Do Lines and Scores Come From?

If you have a Direct Connection Book, the lines you receive on the Place Wagers screen is coming directly from the Book.  For a Manual Book, the Place Wager screen is not even available.  The Place Wager screen is the only screen in the application that receives lines directly from your Book.

The Record Wager screen for both applications and the Line Monitor on the Book is receiving lines directly from our customized data feed.  Regardless of whether or not you have a Direct Connection Book, the Record Wager screen reflects our lines that we send to you and may not be indicative of those your Book is offering.  For lines that come from us, we choose from a consortium of Las Vegas line feeds.

Scores are also provided to you from a data feed that we get.  These scores are vital to resolving wagers and are refreshed every 20 seconds.  In most cases, the lag time between actual events and our data being received by you is between 30 seconds and 5 minutes.
