xContests

The Contests reflects only currently active contests.  There can be any combination of Daily, Weekly, and/or Monthly contests going on at the same time.  If the tab is green, there's currently an active contest being played.  If the tab is yellow, there is currently no active contest being played, but there will be one beginning within 24 hours.  If the tab is red, there is no currently active contest being played or scheduled to begin within 24 hours.

Upon signing up, you receive a $100 credit line to wager with. Then each day for each Bar, that's all you have to do is place a wager to receive an additional $100 towards your credit line (NOTE: It can be for only $1). Your credit line rolls over day to day and is completely independent of when contests start/end. Your credit line has no maximum and these transactions IN NO WAY affect your balance. Your balance is only affected by the outcome of wagers.  The amount you have to wager with is your credit limit plus your current balance minus your open balance (placed wagers that are not yet closed). That means the better that you do, the more you have to wager with. Just in case you blow it all, we will still fund you with $10 so that you can continue to place wagers. Then you can receive your $100 the next day and get right back into it.

To understand what the numbers in the table are representing, it is determined by game time and if the game for that wager falls within the contest date/time window.  For multi-play wagers, think of the wager itself as the parent and the games within it as the child.  The parent has the amount wagered, potential win, and potential loss information.  Tthe children have the games.  The children (games) control when the information in the parent gets used in the Contest tab.

If there is a game within the multi-play wager that falls within a given current contest, the information from the parent will be included.  If there is not, it won't be included.  Here's an example.
Wager Type:  Parlay 2
Amount Wagered: $100
Potential Win:  $250
Potential Loss:  $100
Game 1 within Parlay 2:  Plays on October 5th (assume that this game DOES NOT close the wager)
Game 2 within Parlay 2:  Plays on October 9th

Daily Contest:  October 4th does not have parent information
Daily Contest:  October 5th does have parent information
Daily Contest:  October 6th does not have parent information
Daily Contest:  October 7th does not have parent information
Daily Contest:  October 8th does not have parent information
Daily Contest:  October 9th does have parent information
Daily Contest:  October 10th (and beyond) does not have parent information

For scheduled contests, they are just that.  They are scheduled to begin.  However, the Bar reserves the right to change, cancel, or postpone any contest that has not officially began yet.  Therefore, scheduled cannot be relied upon.  Currently active contests, though, may not be changed, modified, or altered in any manner until they have ended.

Contest Table:
The contest table offers the following:  Rank, Prize, Nickname, Current Balance, Largest Winning Wager, Available Balance, Winning/Losing, Current Wagered, Potential Payout, and Potential Loss.  Each item may be sorted by clicking on the associated header.  If you are only seeing the Rank, Prize, Nickname, and possibly the Current Balance, it means that the Bar is only offering the rest of the information while you are on their WiFi network.  That means if you bring your laptop up to the Bar, the rest of the table will be displayed within this screen.  It will be automatic and there is nothing you have to do other than being on the Bar's WiFi network.  In this instance and depending on your standing, you may want to go up to the Bar and tailor your wagers accordingly against the competition to better your chances of winning.
1) Rank:  The order of each contestant, as determined by their Current Balance (and possibly Largest Winning Wager also), for the chosen contest type (Daily, Weekly, Monthly)
2) Prize:  The reward/award being offered for the associated Rank
3) Nickname:  The nickname that the contestant chooses to go by
4) Current Balance:  The Current Balance of the contestant.  This reflects closed wagers for games as determined for the individual contest.  For more on what factors determines where a closed wager will be applied, please refer to the Results section.
5) Biggest Winner:  The largest winning wager for the contestant for this particular contest.  This is used to break a tie in the case of the Current Balance being the same for any two or more contestants that are vying for a prize.
6) Available Balance:  The amount that the contestant can wager with.  It is calculated by subtracting or adding the Current Balance (depending on if you're in the red or black) to the Overall Limit and then subtracting the Open Balance from that.
7) Winning/Losing:  Assuming the outcomes remain as they are at that moment, it is the balance of all wagers which currently have games being played
8) Current Wagered:  The amount wagered that is currently open, but has yet to be resolved.
9) Potential Payout:  If all currently open wagers result in losses.
10) Potential Loss:  If all currently open wagers result in wins.

For results of contests that have concluded, refer to the Results section at least 24 hours after a contest ends.
