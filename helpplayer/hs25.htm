xUse with Personal and Bar Books

The GameDay Payoff - Player application is compatible with both GameDay Payoff - Bar and GameDay Payoff - Book.  There really only two differences.

When using the Player with a Bar, all Bar Books must be Direct Connection Books.  When using the Player with personal Books, they may be Direct Connection (recommended) or manual Books.  The second difference is that you will always have access to your Bar Books, but you will lose access to your personal Books if you allow your subscription to expire.
