General

Policy is the limits, rules, and odds that the Book sets for the Player.  Changing Policy only affects new wagers.  Changing Policy will never be applied to an Open or Closed wager.  Policy is derived by one of two ways.  If it came from a Direct Connection Book, it came from him and it can only be modified by him.  When a Direct Connection Book changes your Policy, you will receive a notification that such a change has occurred.  It is then recommended that you close and re-open any screens that the new Policy may affect or just close and re-open the application.  Again, Policy from a Direct Connection Book is not modifiable by the Player.

If the Policy is for a Manual Book, it was entered by you based off of his Policy that he communicated to you and it can be changed by you.  For obvious reasons, it is very important that your Policy represents the same Policy that your Book has.  Because odds and rules are referenced for wager calculations, both sides will only match if you each have the same Policy.

Policy for either a Direct Connection Book or a Manual Book is reflected in the availability of wagers in the Place Wager and Record Wager screens.  After changing Policy, you must close and re-open either of these screens if they were open before the policy change was made.  If something is not available in Policy, then it is not available on these screens.  If a wager type is not available in Policy, it will not be available on these screens.  If money lines or buy points are not available in multi-play wagers, they will not be available on these screens.  If you are offered up to a 5 play parlay in Policy, you are offered only up to a 5 play parlay on the these screens.  Policy being reflected on this screen assures you that what you are placing is available.

IMPORTANT:  Many Policy settings are pre-assigned by us.  They are the most common ones and have been pre-assigned by us in the interest of saving you time.  They, however, are not necessarily indicative of what your Book's Policy is.  Please carefully review all Policy assignments before you are finished.  You may need to change or delete amounts, settings, odds, or behavior to reflect that of the Book.
