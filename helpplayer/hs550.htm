xJuice

Juice is the amount you pay in excess of your loss.  For more on what Juice is, refer to Juice in the Wager Types section of the Help.

This screen will reflect the amount of Juice assigned by the Book.  Each field is the percentage.  Hence, for 10% Juice, you would enter a 10 into one of the fields.

Juice is broken down into straight spreads, straight totals, parlay, teasers, pleasers, and action points.  There is no juice on a money line.  Within each of these wager types, Juice can be assigned for a loss or push with minimums for each.  Minimums mean that either the percent or minimum will be enforced, whichever is higher.  There is never Juice on a win and for action points, the Juice is not per point, but for the entire amount.

Typical Juice amounts are 10% for straight wagers and action points with regard to losses only.  Minimums sometimes are enforced.
