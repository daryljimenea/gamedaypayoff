xHow to Get Started (w bar books)

1)  Make sure you have an internet connection.
2)  Go to Add Book where you will choose between a Bar Book or a Personal Book.
3)  For Bar Books and Personal Books that are Direct Connection, go to the Place Wager screen and get started.  For Personal Books that are either not Direct Connection books or you are not using Direct Connection with that Book, go to the Record Wager screen and manually enter the wagers you placed with them.
4)  After placing or recording your wager, go to the Wager Manager screen and use the tabs at the bottom to choose Review, Open wagers, In Progress wagers, or Closed wagers for individuals or all.  Within each there are a variety of views, filters, and sorting to obtain most any result you desire.
5)  If you have a Bar Book chosen from the dropdown, you can go to the GameDay Bar section of the application and find all the information you will need while participating in that Bars fantasy sports wagering contest(s).
6)  The Pro League section offers all games In Progress and Box Scores for each league sorted by date or team.
7)  For transactions, go to the Balances screen to create them as needed.  Wager balances are computed automatically each day, but you may need to pay, receive, or make an adjustment.

That's It!  You are up and running GameDay Payoff - Player.  Good luck!
