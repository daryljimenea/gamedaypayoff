Parlay Rules

A Parlay is a single wager that links together two or more individual wagers and is dependent on all of those wagers winning together.  For more on what a Parlay is, refer to Parlay Wagers in the Wager Types section of the Help.

Parlays may or may not be offered by your Book.  Then, if they are offered, they may or may not allow money lines in them.  When conditions dictate, there may be a tie (or push) and/or no action for a play(s) within the Parlay.  When such situations arise, this screen allows it to be handled to Book specifications.

Parlays are designated in the Wager Manager by Parlay (#plays).  They are available to all sports and plays within the same Parlay may come from different sports.
