Box Scores

This feature is only available in the Player version of GameDay Payoff.  On this screen you can view box scores or schedules with results.  The box score for any game is available shortly after it becomes final.  While only current seasons are available for each league, there are plans for past seasons to become available.

You can sort by league, date, or team.  Sorting by date within a league will show every game that has been played, that is being played, or that will be played on such a date.  Sorting by team within a league will show every game that has been played, that is being played, or that will be played by such team.

For college football and college basketball, you may see a "Non-Division I" for a team.  The reason being is that at this time, we are only supporting Division I teams.  Any team that is not a Division I team will not be designated by its name, but instead, they will all be designated by "Non-Division I".
