Closed

IMPORTANT:  The filter results here will not necessarily match up with the contest results.  This is because the results here are recorded according to when a wager closes, while contest results are determined by the start time of the game that closes out the wager.

The Closed tab shows all wagers that the Book has that are currently resolved.

Wagers in the Closed tab may be one of 3 colors.  They represent the following:
1)  Dark Green - Wager is Won.
3)  Dark Red - Wager is Lost.
4)  Dark Yellow - Wager has Pushed.

A total wager that is currently being played may be resolved once the total is reached.  Meaning, once a total is reached, an over will be closed with a win and an under will be closed with a loss even though the game is still be played.

Any wager that has a red "X" next to it may be deleted.  This is a wager that was entered through the Record Wager screen.  If there is not a red "X" next to the wager, it was placed and accepted through the Place Wager screen via Direct Connection Book and cannot be deleted.

For the filter, going back "0" days represents the "Today".  Going back one day would be "today" and "yesterday".  That pattern follows when going back days.
