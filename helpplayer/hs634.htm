Balances

The Balances screen is the financial transaction center of the application.  It is here that the opening balance, daily wager totals, payments made to the Book, amounts received from the Book, and adjustments are recorded.

Wagers are recorded directly from the Wager Manager's Closed tab.    The three other transaction types are entered by hitting the "Create New Transaction" button.  This button is only accessible if a Book is chosen from the dropdown menu.

The four transaction types are detailed as follows:

Wagers (automatic):  These transactions are transferred daily from the Closed tab in the Wager Manager.  They cannot be manipulated or deleted here, but the Closed tab is influenced by the Place Wager and Record Wager screens.  Any changes made from those screens, have a direct result on the Closed tab of the Wager Manager, and hence, the Balances.  Hitting the Wagers link in the Transaction column of the Balance List will provide you with a screen showing the wagers that determined that daily amount.

Paid (manual):  These transactions are manually entered by using the "Create New Transaction" button.  You may add comments to the transaction and these types of transactions may be deleted from the Balance List.

Received (manual):  These transactions are manually entered by using the "Create New Transaction" button.  You may add comments to the transaction and these types of transactions may be deleted from the Balance List.

Adjustments (manual):  These transactions are manually entered by using the "Create New Transaction" button.  Use this transaction when you and the Book have different balances and just cannot resolve it unless an Adjustment is made that is outside of paying, receiving, or wagering totals.  You may add comments to the transaction and these types of transactions may be deleted from the Balance List.

For Direct Connection Books, the current record of balance is coming directly from him.  This way, you will always know if the balance you both show is in sync or not.  Checking the Group by Book checkbox will give you an aggregate of all transactions within the time range specified.  If All is chosen from the Book dropdown list, there will be an aggregate for each Book you have.

IMPORTANT:  If the Balance between a Direct Connection Book and you are different, there are two reasons why the Book would have a different balance than you.  The first reason being that your transactions are not the same on the Balances screen.  The Balances screen allows for you to receive, pay, or make adjustments.  If these transactions do not match, the balances will not be the same.

The second reason would be from the Book and you entering different (or failing to enter) wagers on the Record wager screen.  If a wager was taken manually instead of through a Direct Connection, both of you would had to have entered that wager on the Record Wager screen.  If different wagers were entered or not entered at all, the Book would be showing a different balance than you.  Wagers using the Place Wager screen would be the same on both sides and would, therefore, not be the cause of the Book having a different balance than you.
