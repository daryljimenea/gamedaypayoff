Internet Connection Needed?

Many features are internet dependent.  It is strongly recommended that you use a high speed internet connection.  Scores are sent, and thus wagers resolved, from the data feed via the internet.  Lines are sent through the internet either from the Book or us.  Direct Connection Book's send their balance and policy changes while Direct Connection Player's send their wagers through the internet.  Understand, however, that a Player does not need to be on the internet or have the application running at the time a Policy change is made.  If a change is made to Policy while a Player is not connected to the internet, the very next time the Player launches the application and are internet connected, the Policy changes will be made and the Player will be notified.  We stress to you that you must have an internet connection to affectively use GameDay Payoff.
