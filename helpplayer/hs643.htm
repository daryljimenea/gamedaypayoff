xResults

IMPORTANT:  The contest results here will not necessarily match up with the closed results in the Wager Manager.  This is because the results here are determined by the start time of the game that closes out the wager, while the results in the closed tab of the Wager Manager are determined by the end time of the game that closes out the wager.

The Results section reflects only closed contests.  It provides you with a history of previously open contests.  The results of any and all contests will automatically become available to you 24 hours after a contest closes.

Contest results rely on game times and not closing times of wagers. For instance, with recurring contests such as a Daily contest that keeps repeating, it's the game time of the wager and not the end time that determines which Daily contest the wager is in. That means if you're $25 out of 1st place for a Daily contest that's just about to end, get your new wager(s) in quick for a game that is just about to start before the contest ends. Regardless of when the game ends, the results will count and might put you over the top.

For Multi-Play wagers (Parlay, Teaser, and Pleasers), it is the game time of the game that is responsible for closing out the wager that is used to determine which contest(s) the wager counts in.  If you have a 5 play parlay and it's the 4th game that closes out the wager with a loss, it is the start time of that wager that determines which contest(s) the wager counts towards.

There are many filtering options provided to you.  They are as follows:

Nickname:
Choose All or an individual player to return results on.

Display my results on the top:
If you choose to have your results displayed at the top, you do lose some sorting options.

Show records with prizes only:
Use this to decide if you only want to see prize winners or everyone in the contest.

Contest:
Choose one contest type or several to filter your results by.

Start Date OR From/To:
If you choose one contest, you will be presented with the Start Date for any specific contest within the Daily, Weekly, or Monthly that you have chosen.  If you choose two or all three contests, you may specify a date range to filter your results by.

Total Balance:
This is the total balance of all balances that are presented in the table.

Filter:
When you have decided on all of your parameters, hit the filter button to populate the table according to your specifications.

Table:
The table displays the type of contest, rank, prize, nickname, and balance.  You may sort the table any which way you would like just by hitting the header you are interested in sorting by.
