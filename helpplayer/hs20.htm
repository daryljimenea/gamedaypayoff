What is GameDay Payoff?

GameDay Payoff is new technology applied to an outdated industry.  With intended uses in mind (see "Intended Use" topic in Help), sports wagering finally has a software product that treats your profession or hobby with the innovation and sophistication that is applied to all other industries.

GameDay Payoff provides several breakthroughs to sports wagering.  One such breakthrough is the ability to Book with whomever you would like and still use this software to track wagers and get real-time wager status.  However, most notably is the ability of the Book to broadcast lines and receive wagers directly from his Players and for the Player to receive updated lines and place wagers directly from his Book without ever picking up the phone.  With GameDay Payoff - Book version on one end and GameDay Payoff - Player on the other end, you keep your current Book and you keep your current Players.  Nothing changes accept for bringing mistake free efficiency into your operation.  Having a Direct Connection through the internet from one computer to another gives you the power of computing with the benefits of connecting.  With computing and connecting, what once seemed impossible becomes ordinary.   Your new list of ordinary follows:
