User Profiles

GameDay Payoff  - Player application was designed to be used for multiple players.  User Profiles is where you manage who has access to the application and what kind of access they have.  At any given time, there can only be one user logged in at a time.  Upon launching the application for the first time, you will have access to everything as an administrator.  As an administrator, you will always have access to all features within the application, including this User Profiles dialog.  The difference between an administrator and just a regular user is access to this dialog.

At anytime, you may want to password protect the application as an administrator.  To do so, either go to "Change Password" underneath the Player menu or go to this dialog.  Through this dialog, make sure "Admin" is highlighted and hit the "Change Password" button.  Once you have the "Change Password" dialog up, enter your password, confirm it, and provide a hint.  It is strongly advised that you enter a password and associated hint that no one will know but you.  At anytime after you have a password, you may revert back to no password requirement (or blank) by repeating this process and checking "Use Blank Password" from the Change Password dialog.

IMPORTANT:  The password you enter is case sensitive.  Also, if you forget your password when the application is password protected, you will NEVER GAIN ACCESS to the application.  There is no hidden password or means of getting around a forgotten password.  You will have to do a full uninstall (see topic "Uninstall Book/Player" in Help) of the application and start everything all over again.

For all other users, they may be assigned with administrative privileges or not.  Again, the difference between the two is access to User Profiles.  When setting up a new user, hit the "Add User" button.  Give the user a unique user name (no two can have the same name) and decide their privileges.  Either enter their password information or let them.  If you set them up with a password, they can always change it later.  To change their username or password, hit the "Change Profile" button and make such changes.  To delete a user, highlight them and hit the "Delete User" button.

IMPORTANT:  Deleting a user is permanent.  Their profile, settings, and history can never be recovered.

IMPORTANT:  The Administrator and any subsequent users that are assigned as Administrators have access to the User Profile and can, therefore, change anything and everything regarding users.  This includes changing names, passwords, deleting, or adding users.  Even the original Administrator may be deleted or have their password changed.

IMPORTANT (Regular User):  If you are a regular user, any user with Administrator privileges may lock you out by changing your password and/or delete you.  They can even change your password and then gain access to your account.  If you do not want to risk being in such a situation, please get your own GameDay Payoff and retain rights as an administrator.

A user lacking Administrative privileges may change their password at anytime, but cannot change their user name.  You can change your password by going under the Player menu and choosing "Change Password".

When finished using the application, log out or exit the application.  Either choice will end your session and require a password to re-enter if you are a password protected user.
