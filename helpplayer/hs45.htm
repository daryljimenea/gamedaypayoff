Becoming Your Own Book

If you are thinking about forming your own fantasy sports wagering league, you should allow GameDay Payoff to be your tool in running it.  With Direct Connection, it runs itself.  In evaluating just how easy it would be to run your own league, you should consider giving Direct Connection a trial run yourself.  You can do this without having to recruit any Players in order to get started.

To experience Direct Connection yourself, you will need to install the Book application.  If you do not have the Book application already installed, refer to Have Book/Player, Want Other.  Once there, you will refer to the help section entitled "Try Direct Connection Yourself First".

The process required to experience Direct Connection yourself is extremely easy to accomplish and we walk you through each step of the way.  After you experience it, we feel quite confident that your decision to form your own fantasy sports wagering league will already be made.
