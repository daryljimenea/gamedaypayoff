Book Maintenance

The Book Maintenance screen allows you to do delete a Book, change their name, change their type from Manual to Direct Connection, or re-register with a Direct Connection Book.  More details on each follows:

Delete a Book
Deleting a Book completely eliminates that Book's records and existence from throughout the application.  Deleting a Book is PERMANENT and cannot be reversed so give careful consideration before doing so.

Name Change
Click on the Book's name to bring up a dialog that will allow you to change the name of that Book.  You cannot give a Book the same name as one that already exists.

View Book Type
A Book is either a Direct Connection Book (recommended) or a Manual Book.  For more on the benefits of using a Direct Connection Book, see "Why Use a Direct Connection?" in the Help section.

Re-Register
If you are showing a red ball in the lower left hand corner for a Book, that means that he is failing to authenticate you as a registered Player with him.  This could be due to unforeseen technical difficulties or he has decided to revoke your Direct Connection privileges by making you Inactive.  If he has revoked your privileges, the Book setting your status back to Active will result in authentication.  As soon as you know you are Active again, close the application and re-open it.  You should have a green ball with the Book and be authenticated.  If this does not occur and you are still not authenticated, then have him send you another Invite and Re-Register.

WARNING:  When re-registering, make sure that you assign the Invite to the correct Book.

Register
Use Register to turn a Manual type Book into a Direct Connection type Book.  In order for you to use a Direct Connection Book, they must have GameDay Payoff and had sent you an invitation file and password.  Assuming you have an invitation file and password from your Book, hit the "Browse for invitation" button and find your ".gdp" file that the Book sent to you.  Then enter the password that he provided to you and hit the "Next" button.  The password is case sensitive so make sure you enter it correctly.  If all went well, you will have finished the invite process and you now have a Direct Connection with this Book.  A congratulations will show and a "Notification" will pop up in the lower right of the screen.  The notification lets you know whenever the Book changes their Policy on you.  Because that is such an important piece of information, you are informed of changes immediately.  When you establish your initial Direct Connection with the Book, this notification showed up because you received your initial Policy from them and you can go to Policy to view it.  If the process fails, which is indicated by a "Connection Timeout", it means that your Book is currently not available.  The Book could be offline or behind a firewall and unable to broadcast.  You should give the Book a call and resolve it.  If you get an "Invalid password provided" error, re-enter your password (case sensitive) and try again.  If it still does not work, have your Book Retract that invitation and issue you another one.

WARNING:  You can always change a Book from a Manual Book to a Direct Connection Book through the Book Maintenance screen.  However, once your Book is a Direct Connection Book, you cannot change them to a Manual Book.  You can still do everything with that Book accept change Policy (because that comes directly from the Book).  If you need your Direct Connection Book to be a Manual Book, you must delete that Book and start over.  It is highly recommended that you write down the balance you have and any rules/odds settings before deleting.
