Book Status (Ball Colors)

The ball colors represent the status of the book.  They are as follows:
  • Green:  The Book is connected and you are authenticated.  This means the Book recognizes you and you recognize the Book.  Wagering is a go!
  • Red:  If you are showing a red ball in the lower left hand corner for a Book, that means that he is failing to authenticate you as a registered Player with him.  This could be due to unforeseen technical difficulties or he has decided to revoke your Direct Connection privileges by making you Inactive.  If he has revoked your privileges, the Book setting your status back to Active will result in authentication.  As soon as you know you are Active again, close the application and re-open it.  You should have a green ball with the Book and be authenticated.  If this does not occur and you are still not authenticated, then have him send you another Invite and Re-Register.
  • Gray:  The Book is offline so no wagering can take place.
  • No Ball:  Books that you have never established a connection with do not have any balls associated with their status.
