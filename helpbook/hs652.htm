Refer A Friend

IMPORTANT:  You cannot be referred if you have already successfully been referred and received your 2 weeks, made 2 unsuccessful attempts, taken longer than 34 days from your initial install, or have taken longer than 4 days from your initial payment.  Your Refer A Friend may have even disappeared or never even appeared given which ever situation has been realized.  HOWEVER, you may always refer others and they will use your email address for you to receive credit.

You Are the User Providing the Referral to Your Friend:
If you have already made a payment for the data feed (even one), you may not need to ever make another payment again. Just tell a friend to enter the email address we currently have on file through your PayPal billing and we will extend your data feed expiration date by 2 weeks upon their first payment to us. You can tell as many friends as you would like and keep receiving extra free weeks.

You Are the User Receiving the Referral from Your Friend:
If you are within 34 days of initially installing the application AND within 4 days of your initial payment to us, you qualify to receive 2 weeks free just by entering the email address of record from the friend that referred you. As long as that friend has made at least one payment to us, both of you will receive 2 weeks worth of data for free. After you "Pay Apply", a "Refer A Friend" link will appear the NEXT TIME YOU OPEN THE APPLICATION. Just follow the instructions and once your friend is verified as a paying customer, both you and your friends data feed expiration dates will be moved back 2 weeks for free.

Referring a Bar:
To receive your 1 year extension, just get a Bar to sign up for GameDay Payoff - Bar and use the email address that we have on file for you.  Let them know that they will have a place to enter your email address.  Once they do it and we process it, the next time you open your application, your date will be extended for an entire year.  You can sign up as many Bars as you can and the date will just keep getting pushed back.
