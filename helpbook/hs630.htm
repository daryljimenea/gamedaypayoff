Action Points

Action Points is a wager where the winnings or losses are determined by how many points a team does or doesn't cover the point spread by or the total.  For more on what Action Points are, refer to Action Point Wagers in the Wager Types section of the Help.

Action Points may or may not be offered by you.  Even if you do offer them, they may be offered for Spreads, Totals, or both.  The cap is the range allowed and has a minimum and a maximum for points allowed.  The cap will be the same for both Spread wagers and Totals if they are both offered.
