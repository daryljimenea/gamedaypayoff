In Progress

The In Progress tab shows only the Open wagers that currently have a game being played that will affect that wager.

Postponed/Suspended Games:  If a game does not start or finish, you may have wagers that are unresolved.  For such occurrences, you may choose an Action and Apply that action to a play within a multi-play wager or to a straight wager.  Your action will then appropriately close the wager.

Wagers in the In Progress tab may be one of 5 colors.  They represent the following:
1)  Dark Green - Play is Won.
2)  Light Green - Wager or play within a wager is winning.
3)  Light Red - Wager or play within a wager is losing.
4)  Dark Yellow - Play has Pushed
5)  Light Yellow - Wager or play within a wager is pushing

Neither a wager or play within a wager can be dark red because than the wager would be lost and in Closed.

Any wager that has a red "X" next to it may be deleted.  This is a wager that was entered through the Record Wager screen.  If there is not a red "X" next to the wager, it was accepted by you via their Direct Connection with you and cannot be deleted.

The refresh indicates the last time you received an update from us.  Lines and scores are updated from us every 20 seconds.  In most cases, the lag time between actual events and our data being received by you is between 30 seconds and 5 minutes.

NOTE:  The winning/losing in bold may not match the totals on this tab because a wager may be Open (in the Open tab) without anything In Progress at the time.  Such Open wagers will always be a multi-play wager with a play that has a push or a win in it.  Realistically, you are currently winning (or pushing) a multi-play wager that has a win in it even though no play is currently in progress.  The winning/losing amount will be reflecting those wagers in Open that have nothing in progress, but a win plus those wagers showing on the In Progress tab.
