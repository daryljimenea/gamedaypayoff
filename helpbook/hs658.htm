Payment

IMPORTANT:  To use GameDay Payoff - Book, it is required that you receive a data feed from Sports Vice Solutions. After your 10 day trial period, you are charged for receiving this data feed. Please use this screen to purchase the feed. Upon hitting the Buy Now button below, you will be immediately directed to PayPal's secure credit card processing website in order to complete the transaction. Through PayPal's secure credit card processing service, we accept ALL MAJOR CREDIT CARDS, DEBIT CARDS, AND PAYPAL PAYMENTS. Upon your payment being successfully processed, you will be notified as such through the email address that you are required to provide to PayPal. If you are running the Book application and the Player application from the same computer, you will only be required to purchase one data feed subscription and it will be applied to both applications.

Hitting the Payments link will bring you to our secure PayPal credit card processing service.  Go through each screen to make your payment and once you have made a successful payment come back to the application and hit the "Pay Apply" link.  If you have both applications, but on different computers, you will need to pay twice and go through the "Pay Apply" screens on each application. After you submit the email address associated with your PayPal credit card processing payment, your expiration date for the data feed will be moved back accordingly and you will notice the change the NEXT TIME YOU OPEN THE APPLICATION. You MUST go through this process each and every time you make a payment to us.
