VERY IMPORTANT!!!

If you are using the Book for fantasy, you will need to be able to broadcast lines and receive wagers.  To do this, you will need to receive a signal that allows your Players to know that you are available.  This signal is only identified and accepted from your invited Players, but it must be able to get in.  Your Book will NOT work if you are at a location that cannot receive this signal.  This may require little to no action and your Players will NOT need to do anything at all for this to work.  ONLY on your end will something have to be done.  For more on this, see "Direct Connection Books Must Receive Signal" in the Help.

Once you are able to broadcast to your Direct Connection Players, it is important to know that whenever you open the application, the status of accepting wagers or not will ALWAYS be in the "Accepting Wagers from All Players" mode.  This toggle between accepting or not is located in the tool bar next to the Player dropdown list.  If you do not want to accept wagers, just click it.  It will change to "Not Accepting Wagers from Any Players".  During this mode, you will still broadcast lines, Policy, and balances, but will not be taking wagers.  Keep in mind that when you are "Not Accepting Wagers from Any Players", they are unaware of this status until they try to send you one.

Lastly, do not allow your computer to Sleep or Hybernate.  Applications stop running when your computer goes into sleep or hybernate mode.  You can just turn monitor off if you wish.

If you wish to use the Direct Connection yourself before you extend this to your Players, you may do so by keeping the signal internal to your computer.  More on this can be found in Try Direct Connection Yourself First.
