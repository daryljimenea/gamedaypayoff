Where Do Lines and Scores Come From?

The lines are provided to you from a customized data feed that we put into place.  We choose from a consortium of Las Vegas line feeds.  These lines are sent from us into the application through the Line Monitor.  It is through this Line Monitor that your Direct Connection Players receive their lines directly from you.  Manual Players receive our lines and will, therefore, be different from any changes you make to the Line Monitor.  Internally, your Record Wagers screen will also reflect the lines displaying on the Line Monitor.  However, the Record Wager's screen must be closed and re-opened or incur a Player switch for line changes made on the Line Monitor to be reflected.

Scores are also provided to you from a data feed that we get.  These scores are vital to resolving wagers and are refreshed every 20 seconds.  In most cases, the lag time between actual events and our data being received by you is between 30 seconds and 5 minutes.
