Totals

A totals bet is a type of straight wager that considers the score of both teams involved in a game.  When you bet on totals, you are betting on whether the total points scored in the game will be higher or lower than the posted total.

Over/Under Wagers (Totals)
Example: Falcons v. Oilers Total: 35

If a wager is placed on the UNDER (35) the total combined score of both teams must fall under 35 points to win the wager.

If you place a wager on the OVER (35) the combined scores of both teams must fall over 35 points for you to win the wager.

In the event of the total combined scores landing on 35, the wager is a "push' resulting in "no action"
