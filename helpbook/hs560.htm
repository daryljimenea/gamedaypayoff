Buy Points

Buying Points is a way for the Player to move the line in their favor.  For more on what Buy Points is, refer to Buying Points in the Wager Types section of the Help.

Buy Points may or may not be offered by you.  Each field is the percentage.  Hence, for 10% Juice, you would enter a 10 into one of the fields.

If offered, you can have them in spreads and totals within straight, parlay, teaser, and/or pleaser wagers.  The table indicates how much extra Juice will be added onto the wager depending on how many points were bought.

In football, moving onto and off of 3 or 7 points sometimes comes at a premium.  This amount is in addition to the regular charge attached to the point buying itself.  Additionally, it is very important to note that when points bought take the wager through a 3 and/or 7 (onto and off), the additional juice is accumulated.
