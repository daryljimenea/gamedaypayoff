Pleaser Rules

A Pleaser is a group of spread bets or totals combined into one wager whereby each play has points moved against you.  For more on what a Pleaser is, refer to Pleaser Wagers in the Wager Types section of the Help.

Pleasers may or may not be offered by you.  Money Lines are never allowed in them.  When conditions dictate, there may be a tie (or push) and/or no action for a play(s) within the Pleaser.  There may also be a situation where a 2 play Pleaser will be reduced to one game through a push or no action.  When such situations arise, this screen allows it to be handled to your specifications.

Pleasers are designated in the Wager Manager by Pleaser (#plays),(#points).  At this time, they are only available to football.  However, plays within a Pleaser may be mixed between college and pro.
