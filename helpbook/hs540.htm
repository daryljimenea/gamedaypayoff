Limits

These are the limits that you are placing on your Players.  There is an overall limit, a per wager limit, and a maximum payout per wager limit.

The per wager limit may play a critical role in how your lines move.  Even though a Player may keep placing the same wager for the maximum amount until they get the total amount they are seeking on the wager, if you have a per wager limit, you will be able to move the line if the desired amount of out of balance is reached.  A per wager limit will assist in keeping your games in balance.

The maximum payout per wager overrides all other conditions.

The "Payment due when" does not prompt a visual or audible notification, but serves as a reference for what was agreed upon.
