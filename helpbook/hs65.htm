Credit Players for Using Direct Connect

As a Book running your fantasy sports wagering league, you should consider paying the monthly fee for your Players if they choose to use the Direct Connection feature.  This way, you will probably have more participation and definitely less headaches.  The monthly credit should be enough persuasion to make your life a whole lot easier in running your league.  Besides, that monthly fee you'll absorb from each Player should be covered by their league contributions to you anyway?  Think about it.  Right now, there are two methods you may use to do this.

1)  You can make an adjustment in the Balance screen and just lower their overall contributions by the monthly charge for the GameDay Payoff data feed.  With this method, they will be paying us directly, but you will be extending them a credit.
2)  You can pay us directly by attaching their email address to a payment made to us.  This method will require you to make the payment to PayPal credit card processing on their behalf and by using their email address.  Hence, they will receive the notification from PayPal that a payment has been made for them.  They may then go through the normal process with us to have their expiration date moved back.  HOWEVER, they must have made at least one payment to us directly in order for us to have matched up their application with a payment received on their behalf.

WORKING ON:
We are working on two much better methods for you to credit your Players for the data feed they must receive.  The date expected for this features is not known at this time so it would best be recommended that you exercise one of the two methods above to give further incentive to your Players to get them on Direct Connection.
1)  Adjustment to Multiple Players with One Entry Within Application: We will be adding a feature to the balances screen whereby you may make an adjustment to every account at once. This would be handy if you were to credit your Player's for the data feed. Right now, you should consider still doing it, but manual entry for each Player is required.
2)  Paying for Each Player's Data Feed Through PayPal Credit Card Processing: You will be provided an easy means of paying for your Player's data feeds all at once by using our credit card processing through our website.
