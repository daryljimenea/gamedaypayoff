Pleaser Wagers

A pleaser is a group of spread bets or totals combined into one wager.  There are no money lines in a pleaser.  The difference between a pleaser and a teaser is that with a pleaser, the line you bet against for each individual wager is moved against you instead of for you.  If the regular line is -10 and you play a 6-point pleaser, then your line would be -16.  For this reason, payouts are very high as compared to teasers and parlays, but accordingly, your chances of winning are much less.

Pleaser wagers will have spread or totals within them, but not money lines.  Typically, buying points is also allowed.
