Manual Player Instead of Notepad

As an alternative to using the Direct Connection, you can use the GameDay Payoff - Book application to still replace your spiral notebook and pen.  Most likely, you communicate with this Player via the phone, email, or maybe a web browser.  Manually entering the wagers into GameDay Payoff when using these types of Book's allows you to track all wagers in real-time, evaluate lines, keep your accounting current, and operate more efficiently.
