Change Password

You can change your password under the Book menu.  You are defaulted to a blank password, but may put one in.  Once you have the "Change Password" dialog up, enter your password, confirm it, and provide a hint.  It is strongly advised that you enter a password and associated hint that no one will know but you.  At anytime after you have a password, you may revert back to no password requirement (or blank) by repeating this process and checking "Use Blank Password" from the Change Password dialog.

IMPORTANT:  The password you enter is case sensitive.  Also, if you forget your password when the application is password protected, you will NEVER GAIN ACCESS to the application again.  There is no hidden password or means of getting around a forgotten password.  You will have to do a full uninstall (see topic "Uninstall Book/Player" in Help) of the application and start over.
