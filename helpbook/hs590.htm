Teaser Rules

A Teaser is a group of spread bets or totals combined into one wager whereby each play has points moved in your favor.  For more on what a Teaser is, refer to Teaser Wagers in the Wager Types section of the Help.

Teasers may or may not be offered by you.  Money Lines are never allowed in them.  When conditions dictate, there may be a tie (or push) and/or no action for a play(s) within the Teaser.  There may also be a situation where a 2 play Teaser will be reduced to one game through a push or no action.  When such situations arise, this screen allows it to be handled to your specifications.

Teasers are designated in the Wager Manager by Teaser (#plays),(#points).  At this time, they are only available to football.  However, plays within a Teaser may be mixed between college and pro.
