Pro League Not in Book

If you are running the Book application only, you do not have the Pro League section that is only present in the Player application.  The Pro League section in the Player application offers you two features.  They are Box Scores and Games In Progress.

On the Box Scores screen you can view box scores or schedules with results.  The box score for any game is available shortly after it becomes final.  While only current seasons are available for each league, there are plans for past seasons to become available.  While in the Box Scores, you can sort by league, date, or team.  Sorting by date within a league will show every game that has been played, that is being played, or that will be played on such a date.  Sorting by team within a league will show every game that has been played, that is being played, or that will be played by such team.

The Games In Progress screen shows all games that are currently being played.  The information on this screen is updated every 20 seconds.  In most cases, the lag time between actual events and our data being received by you is between 30 seconds and 2 minutes.  You can sort by league and choose to display one or more leagues at a time.  Once a game becomes final, it will move from this screen to the box scores screen.
